Industry Playbooks

Each industry gets a configured workflow: response + qualification + routing + scheduling + follow-up + reputation. Below are common real-world scenarios we implement.

General Business

Lead Response + Scheduling

Ideal for inbound inquiries, quote requests, and consultation scheduling.

Real scenario
A prospect submits a “Request a Quote” form at 7:18 PM.
  • AI responds instantly via SMS/email with 2–4 qualifying questions.
  • Creates the record, tags intent, and routes to the right owner.
  • Books a consult call and sends reminders + prep questions.
  • If no response, runs a follow-up sequence over 48 hours.
Outcome: fewer missed leads, consistent follow-up, faster booking.

Home Services

After-hours Capture + Dispatch

HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, landscaping, restoration.

Real scenario
An emergency request comes in after-hours for “no heat” during winter.
  • AI captures details (address, issue severity, photos if available).
  • Routes to on-call tech and confirms dispatch window.
  • Sends appointment confirmations + pre-visit checklist.
  • Triggers review request after completion and flags negatives for escalation.
Outcome: higher close rate on urgent leads, reduced admin load.

Agents & Brokers

Qualification + Appointment Set

Real estate, insurance, lending, and broker-driven sales.

Real scenario
A lead requests a quote or information on a listing from an ad.
  • AI responds instantly and qualifies budget/timeline/needs.
  • Books call/showing and sends a reminder sequence.
  • Summarizes the conversation and preps the agent with context.
  • Runs nurture follow-ups automatically if not ready today.
Outcome: faster speed-to-lead, more appointments, higher show rate.

Pet Services

FAQs + Booking

Grooming, boarding, training, veterinary clinics.

Real scenario
A customer asks about availability, pricing, and required vaccinations.
  • AI answers FAQs and collects pet + service details.
  • Books dates/appointments and sends prep instructions.
  • Automates reminders, drop-off notes, and post-visit follow-up.
  • Requests reviews and monitors sentiment for proactive recovery.
Outcome: fewer interruptions for staff, more confirmed bookings.

Personal Services

Campaigns + Retention

Salons, med spas, fitness, wellness, professional services.

Real scenario
A business runs a monthly promo and needs bookings fast.
  • AI follows up with leads and answers service questions instantly.
  • Books consultations and sends reminder sequences.
  • Automates reactivation messages for past customers.
  • Captures feedback + reviews after appointments.
Outcome: more promo conversions, stronger retention.

Automotive Services

Appointment Set + Follow-up

Dealerships, repair shops, detailers, tire & service centers.

Real scenario
A customer requests a service appointment or asks about a vehicle listing.
  • AI responds instantly with details and availability.
  • Books a test drive/service slot and sends confirmations.
  • Runs follow-ups if the customer goes quiet.
  • Requests reviews post-service and routes issues to a manager.
Outcome: higher show rate, faster response, stronger reputation.
