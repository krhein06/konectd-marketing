Industry Playbooks

One card per industry. Each card shows a real workflow we implement: response → qualify → route → schedule → follow-up → reputation.

General Business

Lead Response + Scheduling

Best for inbound inquiries, quote requests, and consultation booking.

Real scenario
A prospect submits a “Request a Quote” form at 7:18 PM.
  • AI responds instantly by SMS/email with qualifying questions.
  • Creates the record and routes to the right owner.
  • Books a consult call and sends reminders + prep questions.
  • Runs a follow-up sequence if the lead goes quiet.
Outcome: fewer missed leads and faster bookings.

Home Services

After-hours Capture + Dispatch

HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, landscaping, restoration.

Real scenario
A “no heat” emergency comes in at 9:30 PM.
  • AI captures job details and urgency.
  • Routes to on-call tech and confirms a dispatch window.
  • Sends confirmations + pre-visit checklist.
  • Triggers a review request after completion; escalates negatives.
Outcome: more captured revenue after hours.

Agents & Brokers

Qualification + Appointment Set

Real estate, insurance, lending, broker-driven sales.

Real scenario
A lead requests info from an ad or listing page.
  • AI qualifies timeline/budget/needs instantly.
  • Books call/showing and sends reminder sequence.
  • Summarizes the conversation for the agent.
  • Runs nurture follow-ups if not ready today.
Outcome: faster speed-to-lead and more appointments.

Pet Services

FAQs + Booking

Real scenario

Outcome: reduced front-desk workload and more bookings.
