Industry Playbooks

General Business

Lead Response + Scheduling

Best for inbound inquiries, quote requests, and consultation booking.

Real scenario
A prospect submits a “Request a Quote” form at 7:18 PM.
  • AI responds instantly by SMS/email with qualifying questions.
  • Creates the record and routes to the right owner.
  • Books a consult call and sends reminders + prep questions.
  • Runs a follow-up sequence if the lead goes quiet.
Outcome: fewer missed leads and faster bookings.

Home Services

After-hours Capture + Dispatch

HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, landscaping, restoration.

Real scenario
A “no heat” emergency comes in at 9:30 PM.
  • AI captures job details and urgency.
  • Routes to on-call tech and confirms a dispatch window.
  • Sends confirmations + pre-visit checklist.
  • Triggers a review request after completion; escalates negatives.
Outcome: more captured revenue after hours.

Agents & Brokers

Qualification + Appointment Set

Real estate, insurance, lending, broker-driven sales.

Real scenario
A lead requests info from an ad or listing page.
  • AI qualifies timeline/budget/needs instantly.
  • Books call/showing and sends reminder sequence.
  • Summarizes the conversation for the agent.
  • Runs nurture follow-ups if not ready today.
Outcome: faster speed-to-lead and more appointments.

Pet Services

FAQs + Booking

Grooming, boarding, training, veterinary clinics.

Real scenario
A customer asks about availability and vaccination requirements.
  • AI answers FAQs and collects pet + service details.
  • Books dates/appointments and sends prep instructions.
  • Automates reminders + post-visit follow-up.
  • Requests reviews and monitors sentiment.
Outcome: reduced front-desk workload and more bookings.

Personal Services

Campaigns + Retention

Salons, med spas, fitness, wellness, professional services.

Real scenario
A monthly promo needs bookings quickly.
  • AI follows up with leads and answers service questions.
  • Books consultations and sends reminders.
  • Reactivates past customers automatically.
  • Captures reviews after appointments.
Outcome: higher promo conversion and retention.

Automotive Services

Appointments + Follow-up

Dealerships, repair shops, detailers, tire & service centers.

Real scenario
A customer requests service or asks about a listing.
  • AI responds instantly with details and availability.
  • Books test drive/service slot and sends confirmations.
  • Runs follow-ups if the customer goes quiet.
  • Requests reviews post-service and escalates issues.
Outcome: higher show rate and stronger reputation.
