Your portal connects to the tools you already run—so AI can reduce manual work instead of creating more tabs. The examples below reflect common integrations like Google Business Profile, GA4, Search Console, QuickBooks, Shopify, Zapier, and industry systems such as ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro, Mindbody, Dentrix, and more.

Google Business Profile Google Analytics (GA4) Google Search Console QuickBooks Online Shopify Zapier ServiceTitan Jobber Housecall Pro Mindbody Dentrix Microsoft Teams Zoom / Google Meet

Example workflow: Home Services

ServiceTitan / Jobber / Housecall Pro → AI Receptionist → Booking → Follow-up → Review request

  • AI qualifies job type + urgency
  • Creates/updates customer record
  • Books appointment and sends reminders
  • Triggers review campaign after completion

Example workflow: Ecommerce

Shopify → AI Support → Order status / FAQs → Upsell prompts → Reporting

  • AI answers product/order questions
  • Escalates exceptions to a human
  • Captures insights from conversations
  • Tracks outcomes in reporting
